Wind Speed vs. Latitude

There is a very faint positive parabolic relationship hinted at in the relationship between a city's latitude and recorded wind speeds. The relationship is not particularly strong, but there is a suggestion that wind speeds have the potential to be higher as you get closer to the poles, particularly in the Northern Hemisphere.
